Although it may at first appear to be useless since all it actually does is replicate the text formatting of other elements, <address> is useful for informing search engines, screen readers, and any other software attempting to read your page that it is specifically looking at an address.

Example: <p><address>Page created by <a href="mailto:actuallya@fakeemailaddress.com">Violet Rose</a>.<br>Contact me at:<br>notarealwebsite.com<br>Box 404, Nonexistence<br>USA</address></p>

Will be rendered as:

Page created by Violet Rose.
Contact me at:
notarealwebsite.com
Box 404, Nonexistence
USA
